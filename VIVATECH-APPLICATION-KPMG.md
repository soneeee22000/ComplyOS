## Startup Name

**ComplyOS** -- The AI Compliance Autopilot

## One-Line Pitch

ComplyOS replaces months of consulting with an AI agent that classifies, audits, and documents EU AI Act compliance in minutes -- turning a EUR 17B consulting problem into automated software.

## The Problem

The EU AI Act is the most significant AI regulation in history. By August 2, 2026:

- Every high-risk AI system in the EU must have technical documentation, risk assessments, and conformity declarations
- Fines reach EUR 35 million or 7% of global revenue
- Over 50% of organizations don't even know which of their AI systems are in scope

Today's solution? **Hire KPMG or Big 4 consultants** at EUR 50K-500K per engagement. But:

- Consultants can't scale to assess 65,000+ high-risk systems across the EU
- Assessments take months -- the deadline is 4 months away
- Every AI system update requires re-assessment (ongoing cost)

**The consulting model doesn't scale for AI Act compliance. Software does.**

## Our Solution

ComplyOS is an AI agent that automates what compliance consultants do manually:

1. **Inventory** -- Register and catalog all AI systems in your organization
2. **Classify** -- AI agent determines risk level using RAG over the full EU AI Act, with cited legal reasoning
3. **Assess** -- Identify compliance gaps across all Article 9-15 requirements with severity-ranked remediation plans
4. **Document** -- Auto-generate Article 11 technical documentation, risk management plans, and conformity declarations
5. **Monitor** -- Continuous compliance tracking as systems and regulations evolve

**Think of it as Vanta for the EU AI Act.**

## Why This Matters for KPMG

ComplyOS doesn't replace KPMG -- it makes KPMG 100x more effective:

- **Augment consultants:** KPMG teams use ComplyOS to assess clients faster, serve more clients, increase margins
- **Scale advisory:** A single KPMG consultant with ComplyOS can assess 50 AI systems in a day instead of 1 per week
- **Recurring revenue:** ComplyOS's monitoring layer creates ongoing compliance relationships, not one-time engagements
- **First-mover advantage:** The firm that deploys AI Act compliance tooling first captures the market

## Technical Innovation

- **Multi-agent architecture (LangGraph):** Specialized agents for classification, gap analysis, and document generation -- not a single monolithic prompt
- **RAG over the complete EU AI Act:** All 113 articles, 13 annexes, and supplementary guidance indexed and retrievable with source citations
- **Confidence scoring:** Every classification includes a confidence score and legal reasoning chain, so humans can verify edge cases
- **European-sovereign:** Built in Paris, data stays in EU, GDPR-compliant by design
