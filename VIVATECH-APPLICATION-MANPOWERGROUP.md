## Startup Name

**ComplyOS** -- AI-Powered EU AI Act Compliance for Workforce Technology

## One-Line Pitch

ComplyOS is an AI agent that automatically classifies, audits, and certifies workforce AI tools for EU AI Act compliance -- ensuring companies can deploy AI in hiring without legal risk.

## The Problem

**AI in hiring is classified as HIGH-RISK under the EU AI Act.** Every company using AI to screen, rank, or match candidates in the EU must comply by August 2, 2026 or face fines up to EUR 35 million.

The workforce industry has a massive problem:

- ManpowerGroup and its clients deploy AI across 75+ countries -- but EU compliance requirements are unclear and expensive to assess
- Over 50% of organizations lack systematic inventories of their AI systems
- Current approach: hire consultants at EUR 50K-500K per engagement, wait months for assessments
- There is NO automated tooling for AI Act compliance in the workforce sector

**The irony:** The industry building "Human First, Digital Always" workforce solutions can't deploy them in Europe without solving compliance first.

## Our Solution

ComplyOS is an **AI compliance agent** that:

1. **Scans** -- Ingests descriptions of any AI workforce tool (ATS, AI screeners, skills assessors, matching engines)
2. **Classifies** -- Automatically determines EU AI Act risk level with cited legal reasoning
3. **Audits** -- Identifies exactly which compliance requirements are met and which have gaps
4. **Generates** -- Produces the technical documentation, risk assessments, and conformity declarations required by law
5. **Monitors** -- Tracks compliance drift as regulations and AI systems evolve

**Time to compliance: 15 minutes instead of 3 months.**

## Why This Fits "Human First, Digital Always"

ManpowerGroup's thesis is that AI should give organizations speed and scale without leaving people behind. But here's the reality:

- AI in recruitment is the **single most regulated AI category** in the EU AI Act
- The Act specifically targets AI used in "employment, workers management and access to self-employment" (Annex III, Category 4)
- Without compliance tooling, companies will either (a) stop using AI in hiring (losing speed), or (b) risk massive fines (losing trust)

**ComplyOS is the bridge.** It ensures workforce AI is deployed responsibly, transparently, and legally -- so companies can be BOTH innovative AND compliant. Human first, digital always, legally always.
